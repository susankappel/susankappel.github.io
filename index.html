Creating Community after Tragedy

Art therapy has the power to connect. Developed collaboration between New York schools and Marjorie Stoneman High School, Parkland, Florida. Worked with approximately 1000 high school students and 300 5th and 6th graders in New York to introduce Art Therapy as a healing strategy and engender connection and support in anticipation of first anniversary of school shooting in Parkland Florida.

Coordinator Crisis Counseling Center

Conducted individual and group crisis counseling for hundreds affected by the 9/11 World Trade Center attack. Established a crisis-counseling center for the victims and their families to have over 40 therapists provide treatment and ongoing counseling. Acted as liaison between Aon (50,000 employees worldwide), the conference center, employees, their families and the crisis counselors. 9/01-5/02
